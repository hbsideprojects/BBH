Frequently asked questions

Every hire comes with your Bike Box, a built-in GPS tracker, a full installation manual and video, a pre-race packing checklist, cable ties, an anti-crush joint, and interior padding. Everything you need to pack and travel with confidence.
We offer two box types: our Road/Gravel box fits standard road and gravel bikes, and our TT/Aero box fits TT/aero, MTB, and electric bikes. All bikes need to be broken down per our packing video (wheels off, bars turned, seat post removed). If you're on an unusually large frame or an oversized e-bike, message us your bike's dimensions before booking and we'll confirm fit.
Choose a 7-day hire (£99.95) or a 10-day hire (£135) — whichever suits your race or trip. Need longer? Message us before your return date to arrange an extension at £15/day. Boxes returned late with no extension agreed in advance are charged £50/day, so it's always worth letting us know if your plans shift.
The full hire fee is taken at the point of booking, nothing extra to pay on collection. Your £200 damage deposit is held separately as a security authorisation, not a charge, so it never shows up as money out of your account unless it's actually used.
Pickup is available from partner bike shops in Edinburgh and Glasgow, or from trusted locker locations in Edinburgh — whichever's easiest for you. Every booking confirms your exact location, address, and a map link. At present we only offer collection from locations in Edinburgh and Glasgow, but we're working on expanding — let us know if there's another location you think we should prioritise.
You'll get a confirmation email with a unique confirmation number. At a partner shop, just show or read out your number at the counter. At a locker, enter it to unlock and collect. No paperwork, no waiting around.
Right now, collection and return are only available through our partner shop network in Edinburgh and Glasgow, or trusted lockers in Edinburgh. We don't currently offer delivery or collection outside these areas, but it's something we're actively looking at as we grow — get in touch if you're outside our current network and we'll let you know if there's a workaround.

Return your box to the same location that you picked it up, a partner shop or locker, within your booking's end date. We inspect every return against a fixed checklist, so refunds are fast and fair.
We hold a fully refundable £200 deposit on your card at booking — never charged unless there's damage beyond normal wear and tear. Clean return means your deposit is released within 5–7 business days, no chasing required.
Our boxes are built to withstand the rigours of air travel, but airlines can occasionally cause damage during handling. If this happens, report it to the airline before you leave the airport and get a Property Irregularity Report (PIR). Send us the PIR within 24 hours of your return and your deposit is released in full — we'll pursue the claim with the airline directly. We recommend taking out travel insurance that covers sports equipment as extra peace of mind.
Plans change — here's how refunds work if you need to cancel:

• More than 7 days before collection: full refund
• 3–7 days before collection: 50% refund
• Less than 48 hours before collection: no refund

Your damage deposit is always released in full on cancellation, regardless of timing.
Email us at hello@bikeboxhire.io and we'll get back to you as quickly as we can. If it's urgent — a pickup issue or a live GPS query on the day — WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us.
If your plans shift, message us before your return date and we'll do our best to extend your booking — extensions are charged at £15/day, though they depend on whether the box is already reserved for the next hire, so we can't guarantee availability. If your box isn't returned on time and no extension was agreed in advance, a late return fee of £50/day applies instead.
